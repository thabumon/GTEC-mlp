International Certification courses

QUICKBOOKS CERTIFIED USER [QBCU]

Department of Finance & Business Management

QUICKBOOKS CERTIFIED USER ONLINE (QBCU):

Intuit® QuickBooks, the industry leader in managerial accounting software for entrepreneurs and small business, provides an easy-to-understand platform for students to grasp accounting concepts while honing skills in the most prevalent bookkeeping application in small business today. Students can validate their QuickBooks knowledge by becoming an Intuit QuickBooks Certified User (QBCU).
The Intuit QuickBooks Certified User certification is an industry-recognized credential that effectively validates one’s skills in the Intuit QuickBooks accounting software. Intuit QuickBooks Certified User exam vouchers will now include access to both the Intuit QuickBooks Desktop AND Intuit QuickBooks Online tests so candidates can demonstrate their skills in whatever version of Intuit QuickBooks they prefer.

CERTIFICATION :

The Intuit QuickBooks Certified User Online exam will test candidates on new features found in the Intuit QuickBooks Online software. While many of the features are similar, Intuit QuickBooks Online is a cloud-based subscription service that provides the flexibility to take care of business anytime, anywhere. With the Online version, you are tested on the new features which include: receipt capture from a mobile device, cloud-based app support, work from anywhere on any device, instant file access for accountants, schedule and send invoices automatically.
